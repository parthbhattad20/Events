VENTURERS

PVGCOET is a six-year-old inter-departmental college club now, and with so many years that have gone past, we have learned to master the skill of event management and helping people through their entrepreneurship journey. The E-Cell of our college is ranked amongst the top E-Cells for the E-Summit held each year at IIT Bombay and has been striving to reach new levels as we grow older and more experienced each year.
From the year 2019, we commenced on the new journey of hosting our inter-college annual event called 'Venturers."
